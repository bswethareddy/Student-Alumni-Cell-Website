Prof. Rajat Moona
Director
Dear graduating students,
Warmest congratulations on your well-deserved success! Your enthusiastic journey as a student of IIT Bhilai has reached its destination and I am glad that I made a trace in that exciting journey. From being a student of the institute, you have now become the alumnus of IIT Bhilai. Though the status changes, our relationship shall still continue to be the same. Through intense academic training and various other non-academic activities, I hope we were able to shape you as leaders of science and technology. Be prepared for the greater battle for which the nation is looking forward to a great warrior leading the country in its prosperous future. The institute remained as a provider for your academic, professional and technological hunger throughout your stay on campus and now the time has come for you to contribute your little penny to the institute. I urge you to provide your support in all possible ways to make the institute a better place of learning for your present and future juniors.
